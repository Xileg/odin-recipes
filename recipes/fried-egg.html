Fried egg

Easy breakfast

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Oil or butter
  • Salt and pepper (optional, according to your taste)

Steps

  1. Heat the pan.
  2. Add oil or butter to the pan.
  3. Break the egg and add it to the pan.
  4. Wait until the edges are slightly golden.
  5. Use the spatula to flip the egg.
  6. Once it is done, enjoy it.
